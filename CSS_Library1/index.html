The game

Dishonored 2 is an action-adventure game with stealth elements played from a first-person perspective. You can either play as Corvo Attano from the first game or you can play as Emily Kaldwin, a new playable character. Both characters posesses their own unique powers and abilities. The world will also react depending on what you do and what aproaches you take during missions, your actions have consequences.

The story begins...

Fifteen years after Corvo Attano restored Emily Kaldwin to the throne following the assassination of her mother, Dunwall has prospered under her reign. A serial killer known as "the Crown Killer" has been murdering Emily's enemies, leading many to believe that Emily and Corvo are responsible. During a ceremony in remembrance of Jessamine Kaldwin's assassination, Duke Luca Abele of Serkonos arrives with the witch Delilah Copperspoon. Delilah claims to be Jessamine's half-sister and the true heir to the throne...
